What SmartBPM Includes

Everything You Need to Run
Operations Inside Teams

Six core capabilities, pre-built and ready to configure for your business — deployed as a Teams Add-in or SharePoint app.

✅

Smart Approval Workflows

Multi-level, rule-based approvals for any business request — configured for your team structure and policies.

  • Purchase and expense approvals
  • Leave and HR request management
  • Sales discount authorisations
  • Vendor onboarding approvals
  • Full audit trail and timestamps
📚

Company Knowledge Base

A structured, searchable SharePoint knowledge hub accessible directly from Teams — always the latest version.

  • SOPs and process guides
  • Policies and HR documents
  • Product and price lists
  • Onboarding packs for new staff
  • Role-based access control
🔗

ERP & System Integration

Surface your accounting or ERP data directly in Teams — so your team acts without switching systems.

  • Xero, QuickBooks, Sage connect
  • SAP Business One integration
  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 sync
  • Approved transactions write-back
  • Real-time data visibility
📊

Operations Dashboard

A live, role-based dashboard in Teams showing the numbers and statuses your team needs to stay aligned and take action.

  • Pending approvals at a glance
  • Team KPIs and targets
  • Outstanding tasks and deadlines
  • Stock and inventory alerts
  • Configurable per role or department
📋

Digital Forms & Data Capture

Replace paper forms and email templates with structured digital forms that collect, validate, and route data automatically.

  • Custom form builder
  • Conditional logic and validation
  • Auto-routing to approvers
  • Data stored in SharePoint
  • Mobile-friendly on Teams app
🔔

Smart Notifications & Alerts

Automated, contextual notifications that keep the right people informed — without the noise of broadcast messages.

  • Approval requests to approvers
  • Status updates to requestors
  • SLA escalation reminders
  • System event alerts
  • Delivered via Teams chat or channel
