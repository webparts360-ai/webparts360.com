HRDF-claimable masterclasses for IT-led modern workplaces

Equip your IT, business, and analytics teams to get real value from SharePoint, Power BI, and AI in Microsoft 365. Our HRDF-claimable public and in-house courses are built by practitioners who design and run critical workflow solutions on these platforms every day.

Who this is for

These programmes are designed for IT professionals, modern workplace leads, power users, and business managers who are responsible for Microsoft 365 adoption and need practical skills, not just theory.

  • IT and digital workplace teams rolling out or maturing Microsoft 365.
  • Business owners who sponsor intranets, collaboration sites, and dashboards.
  • Power users who will build lists, pages, flows, and reports for their departments.

All courses are HRDF-claimable and can be delivered as public intakes or in-house sessions for your organisation (subject to HRD Corp guidelines).

Course catalogue

Three focused programmes for real-world Modern Work scenarios

Each course is designed as a short, intensive masterclass with hands-on labs and examples drawn from actual SharePoint, Power BI, and AI implementations. Participants leave with skills they can apply immediately to your environment.

SharePoint Masterclass

A 2-day, hands-on course where participants build a working intranet on Microsoft 365 – combining SharePoint, Teams, and Planner for collaboration, document management, lists, simple automation, and governance.

Duration: 2 days Level: Intermediate Format: Public or in-house

Essential Power BI

A dashboard-in-a-day style introduction to Power BI. Participants learn how to connect and shape data, build interactive reports with drill-through, and publish dashboards that business stakeholders can actually use to make decisions.

Duration: 1 day Level: Introductory Format: Public or in-house

Pragmatic AI series

A 2-day executive and practitioner deep dive into applying AI tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity to specific workloads – including HR analytics, recruitment, L&D, and HR operations – with content tailored to your context.

Duration: 2 days Level: Executive & practitioner Format: Customisable in-house
Learning outcomes

Built for teams who need to deliver, not just attend training

These programmes are designed to align with the way you already use Microsoft 365 – intranets, workflows, dashboards, and people operations – so participants can apply what they learn directly to existing projects.

  • Design and structure SharePoint sites that support real collaboration and document use.
  • Use Power BI to turn operational data into dashboards and drill-through reports.
  • Identify where AI tools can safely and effectively augment HR and business processes.
  • Apply governance and best practices that keep your Microsoft 365 environment manageable.

Course agenda highlights

  • Day 1 – SharePoint Masterclass: Site architecture, pages, lists, permissions, Teams integration.
  • Day 2 – SharePoint Masterclass: Document management, Power Automate approvals, search & governance.
  • Power BI day: Connecting data, modeling basics, building reports, sharing dashboards.
  • AI series: Modern Work AI landscape, use-case design, hands-on labs, policy & risk discussion.

Detailed agendas can be adjusted to match your current Microsoft 365 maturity and project priorities.
