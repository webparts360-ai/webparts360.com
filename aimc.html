What this masterclass focuses on

  • Build an AI HR assistant that answers policy questions with cited, accurate responses from your handbook.
  • Screen candidates faster with AI-supported CV review and skills matching.
  • Automate onboarding emails, schedules, and compliance checklists.

Why this course

AI that is grounded in real HR work, not hype

Many AI sessions stay at the level of demos and inspiration. This masterclass is built around specific HR workflows — recruitment, onboarding, L&D, and operations — so participants leave with ready-to-use patterns.

The HR reality

  • Recruiters and hiring managers drown in CVs and repetitive outreach.
  • Onboarding tasks and policy questions eat into time for strategic work.
  • L&D teams struggle to keep up with content demands across the organisation.
  • People analytics insights often remain trapped in spreadsheets and slide decks.

The AI opportunity

  • Use AI to triage and summarise candidate pipelines in minutes instead of hours.
  • Deploy grounded HR assistants that answer policy and benefits questions with proper citations.
  • Generate learning assets, guides, quizzes, and comms on demand from existing materials.
  • Turn HR and performance data into clear, executive-ready reports and recommendations.
